What is the Redux token?


Redux simply put is another ERC20 utility token. The token is based off a principle of dilution manipulation by reducing the total supply of the token. Redux token accomplishes this by removing a percent of every transaction by volume from the total supply. Another equal a percentage is taken out of circulation to be used for various project needs. A certain percent, based on transaction size, will be taken out of every transaction and removed circulation. This of course, is designed to increase the intrinsic value of the coin.

When is the ICO and how do I buy Redux?


There will not be a classic ICO. You can purchase the token on the listed exchanges in the form of direct IEO. We skipped the ICO stage because of the common frustration of waiting for launch. Furthermore we do not want any delays in development to prevent the use of the token. The generation of the code and listing are considered primary(high priority) goals.Tokens will be sold in lots of 10k each week at the fair market price plus 5% with the exception of first three weeks which will be at 10% above fair market price as a kickstart.The initial lot will be valued at $0.25.This will be the a general standard which we intend to apply.In the event that liquidity is required for a specific need,then the used amount will broken up and subtracted from the lot size to reverse any diluting effects.Example:If we need to give 15,000 RDX to a parnter for liquidity,then the lot size for the next three weeks could be lowered to 5,000 as a counter measure.

How will Reverse dilution effect the price of the coin?


One of its unique features is an automatic burn on each transaction made. One burn per a transaction is continued until the total supply is 500k tokens. initial supply is only 5 million and further restrictions on sells are design to give much needed growth without any diluting effects. The goal is to at least maintain value and provide a psychological barrier against selling.

What about future growth and development?


The current crypto mime revolves around plethora of schemes like mining,minting, pre-pre-ico, pre-ico, ico, IEO and etc. The result is usually the same. Large sums of money raised initially,replaced by the diluting forces of POW/POS and ICO issuers dumping on they're own creation. After everything is said and done,you end up with investors holding a bag for three years;whilst the underlying ICO promises are never delivered on. Why would they deliver on their promises? They already have the money and certainly don't want to spend any of it helping you out. I know that is a bleak view of things, but It is one of the key difference Redux Token offers. We only issue a limited amount of tokens in a given time period. Even if we sold every coin, the main source of revenue is in the burn fee. In other words the more value the token has,the more money the contract holder makes. Plently of incentive to keep things moving and find more use cases. In a situation like that transaction volume is key to profits made off of fees. Because of this the token holder has a steady destruction of supply. I call that a win/win.

Is there a guarantee that the product will maintain it's price?


The general working theory is that dilution is bad for the price of cryptos. The main idea behind this token is to mitigate dilution aggressively. That would normally mean price appreciation. However,Markets have unforeseen events. There is no way to know for sure, what will happen to the price of a product in the future.There are no guarantees of value.Utillity tokens are driven by usage which can be difficult to establish in a meaningful way.Due to the regulatory restrictions we cannot issue a guarantee of any kind as it would classify Redux Token as a security.
