What is the Redux token?

Redux simply put is another ERC20 utility token. The token is based off a principle of dilution manipulation by reducing the total supply of the token. Redux token accomplishes this by removing a percent of every transaction by volume from the total supply. Another equal a percentage is taken out of circulation to be used for various project needs. A certain percent, based on transaction size, will be out of every transaction and removed circulation. This of course, is designed to increase the intrinsic value of the coin.

How will Reverse dilution effect the price of the coin?

The general working theory is that dilution is bad for the price of cryptos. The main idea behind this token is to mitigate dilution aggressively. That would normally mean price appreciation. However,Markets have unforeseen events. There is no way to know for sure, what will happen to the price of a product in the future.
